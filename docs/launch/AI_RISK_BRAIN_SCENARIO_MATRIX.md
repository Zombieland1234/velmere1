Expected:

- Limitations may mention missing holder/contract feeds.
- Verdict stays prescreen/review-aware, not hype.

## B. Stablecoin near peg
Expected:
- Low velocity risk.
- Reserve/issuer/redemption missing data lowers confidence.
- Stablecoin is not treated as a meme token.

## C. Stablecoin depeg
Expected:
- Strong risk floor.
- Clear limitation around issuer/reserve evidence.
- Next action: verify reserve, redemption status, liquidity and exchange depth.

## D. RWA / tokenized fund
Expected:
- Low volatility is not treated as safety.
- Missing issuer/redemption/reserve proof lowers confidence.

## E. Low-float parabolic pump
Expected:

- Verdict says anomaly/requires review, not confirmed scam.

## F. Contract privilege cluster
Expected:
- Mint/pause/blacklist/high tax/proxy increase score.
- Honeypot/transfer restriction style signals create high risk floor.
- Wording stays evidence-safe.

## G. Thin-liquidity exit risk
Expected:
- Pump + weak exit liquidity increases risk.
- Orderbook/slippage missing keeps confidence limited.
- Next action: inspect depth, holders, liquidity zones.

## H. No-data token
Expected:
- Not safe.
- Low confidence.
- Manual review.
- Limitations list supply, holders, contract, orderbook and OSINT gaps.

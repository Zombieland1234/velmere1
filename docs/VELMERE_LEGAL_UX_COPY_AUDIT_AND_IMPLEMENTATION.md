Prepared as senior EU/German e-commerce legal UX copywriting draft for Velmère.

## Assumptions

- Jurisdiction assumption: Germany/EU.
- Brand tone: dark luxury streetwear, restrained, editorial, not hype-driven.
- This is a draft/template pack, not final legal advice.
- A qualified lawyer must verify the final public legal pages, withdrawal wording, Impressum fields, tax/VAT treatment, consumer dispute-resolution wording, data processors and VLM/token structure before launch.
- VLM must stay blocked until security review, legal/MiCA review, registry publication and wallet-safety notices are complete.

### 6. Shipping Policy

### Shipping Policy

**Kicker:** Legal / shipping
**Updated:** Updated: 31 May 2026

**Draft notice:** Draft customer-facing template. Must be completed with the exact seller, tax, fulfilment, payment and data-processing details before launch and reviewed by a qualified lawyer.

Shipping information must be clear before checkout. No payment should be accepted until delivery countries, costs and fulfilment rules are complete.

#### Countries served
Velmère will list the countries served before checkout opens. If a country is not available in checkout, Velmère does not currently ship there.

#### Delivery costs
Delivery costs are shown separately before the customer places an order. Free shipping, if offered, must state the conditions clearly.

#### Delivery estimates
Estimated production and carrier delivery windows are shown before checkout. Made-after-order production time, where used, is separate from carrier delivery time.

#### Carriers and fulfilment partners
Orders may be fulfilled by Velmère directly or by a named fulfilment partner such as Printful or another production partner. The actual carrier may vary by destination.

#### Tracking
When tracking is available, the tracking link is sent by email after the shipment record is created.

#### Failed delivery
Customers must provide a complete and correct delivery address. If a parcel cannot be delivered, Velmère will explain the available options, costs and next steps based on the carrier status and consumer law.

#### Damaged parcels
If a parcel arrives damaged, contact Velmère promptly with the order number, photos of the parcel and photos of the garment. This helps Velmère investigate with the carrier or fulfilment partner. Statutory consumer rights remain unaffected.

#### Customs and import costs
For deliveries outside the EU, customs duties, import taxes, brokerage fees or local charges may apply. These must be disclosed clearly before checkout if Velmère sells outside the EU.

#### No checkout without shipping data
Checkout must remain closed until shipping regions, rates, taxes, delivery estimates, return address and fulfilment rules are configured.


### 7. Returns & Withdrawal Policy

### Returns & Withdrawal Policy

**Kicker:** Legal / returns
**Updated:** Updated: 31 May 2026

**Draft notice:** Draft customer-facing template. Must be completed with the exact seller, tax, fulfilment, payment and data-processing details before launch and reviewed by a qualified lawyer.

This page gives consumer-friendly withdrawal and return information for Germany/EU. Exact wording, exceptions and return address must be verified before launch.

#### 14-day withdrawal right
If you are a consumer in the EU, you generally have the right to withdraw from an online purchase within 14 days without giving a reason. For goods, the period usually starts when you or a person named by you receives the goods.

#### How to withdraw
Send a clear statement that you withdraw from the contract by email to velmere141@gmail.com. Include your name, order number, ordered item and the email address used for the order. A model withdrawal form is provided below, but using it is not mandatory.

#### Model withdrawal form
To: Marcin Bajak, velmere141@gmail.com
I/we hereby withdraw from the contract for the purchase of the following goods:
Ordered on / received on:
Customer name:
Customer address:
Order number:
Date:
Signature only if sent on paper.

#### Return process
After withdrawal, Velmère will provide the return address and instructions. The garment should be returned without undue delay and normally no later than 14 days after the withdrawal notice.

#### Refund timing
Eligible refunds are made using the original payment method unless otherwise agreed. Velmère may wait with the refund until the goods are received back or until the customer provides proof of return shipment, where legally permitted.

#### Return shipping costs
Return shipping costs are borne by the customer only if this was clearly disclosed before purchase and the rule is legally valid. Otherwise, Velmère must follow the legally required cost allocation.

#### Condition of returned goods
Customers may inspect goods as they would in a shop. If goods are handled beyond what is necessary and lose value, Velmère may have a legally permitted claim for diminished value.

#### Exceptions
Exceptions may apply only where legally valid, for example for goods made to the customer’s specifications or clearly personalised. Standard limited drops are not automatically excluded just because stock is limited.

#### Defective or wrong items
If an item is defective, damaged or wrong, contact support with the order number and photos. Statutory conformity and warranty rights remain unaffected.

#### Products and limited drops

#### Order refusal and abuse prevention
Velmère may refuse, cancel or limit orders where required by law, fraud prevention, payment failure, stock error, obvious pricing error, abusive behaviour, bot activity or violation of these Terms. Consumer rights remain unaffected.

#### Liability
Nothing in these Terms excludes liability that cannot be excluded by law, including liability for intent, gross negligence, injury to life, body or health, or mandatory consumer rights. Other liability rules must be reviewed for German/EU law before launch.

#### Governing law
German law may apply where legally valid. Mandatory consumer protection rights in the consumer’s country of residence remain unaffected.

#### Changes to these Terms
Velmère may update these Terms for future use. The version applicable to an order is the version shown before the order is placed.

## D. Developer implementation notes

### Where each legal link should appear

- Footer: Terms, Privacy Policy, Shipping, Returns, Seller Details / Impressum, Contact, VLM Access Notice.
- Cart drawer: Terms, Returns & Withdrawal Policy, Privacy Policy and checkout readiness notice.
- Checkout review step: Terms, Privacy Policy, Shipping, Returns, seller identity and total price.
- Product detail: Shipping/Returns link, size guide, material/care, availability status.
- VLM page and wallet modal: VLM Access Notice and Wallet Safety Notice.
- Square composer: Community Terms/moderation note before posting.

### What must be shown before checkout

- Seller legal identity and full address.
- Main product characteristics: material, size, fit, colour, care, price, currency and availability.
- Total price including taxes or clear tax treatment.
- Delivery costs, delivery methods and delivery estimates.
- Payment provider and accepted payment methods.
- Withdrawal/returns information, return cost responsibility and model withdrawal form.
- Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
- Clear final order button wording indicating payment obligation, where legally required.

### What must be shown before wallet connection

- Wallet connection is optional and separate from clothing checkout.
- Velmère reads public wallet data only after permission.
- Velmère never asks for seed phrase, private key, wallet password or custody.
- Read-only status unless the wallet displays a clear transaction confirmation.
- VLM is access-only, not investment, not public offer, no guaranteed value, no guaranteed launch.

### What should be blocked until legal review/audit is complete

- VLM public sale, claim, airdrop, gated purchase, live token route, contract address publication or protocol fee UI.
- Checkout and payment capture until Impressum, tax, shipping, returns, privacy and fulfilment data are complete.
- Google/OAuth production login until provider keys, privacy disclosures and data-processing details are complete.
- Newsletter marketing until consent flow, unsubscribe flow and processor disclosures are complete.
- Analytics/marketing pixels until consent management is live.
- User posting to Square until moderation/reporting rules and UGC terms are live.

### Data fields required for GDPR / Impressum completion

- Seller legal name, legal form, full postal address, country.
- Email, phone if used/required, responsible person if applicable.
- Business registration court/number if applicable.
- VAT ID or legally reviewed tax note if applicable.
- Consumer dispute-resolution participation statement.
- Controller identity and privacy contact.
- Processors: Stripe, Vercel/hosting, database, email/Resend, analytics, OAuth, wallet providers, fulfilment partners, customer support tools.
- For each processor: purpose, data categories, location, legal basis, retention and processing agreement status.
- Retention periods for account, order, tax/accounting, support, newsletter, cookie consent, wallet, Square moderation and logs.

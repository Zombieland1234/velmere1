# PASS2007 - broad trust / legal / route-state aesthetic logic sweep

## Scope
- Research Lab and Security public trust surfaces.
- Contact and FAQ support surfaces.
- Legal document layout.
- Locale and shared loading states.
- Locale and global not-found states.
- Locale error boundary truthfulness and low-motion behavior.

## Main changes
- Removed an unverifiable claim that the error boundary could guarantee session safety.
- Reduced error entrance motion from 650 ms to 240 ms and removed the large blurred decorative field.
- Added polite loading semantics, stable geometry, solid backgrounds and reduced-motion coverage.
- Reworked the global 404 into a neutral PL/DE/EN language selector.
- Simplified the localized 404 by removing the animated orbit treatment.
- Changed legal pages from stacked cards into one readable document with divided sections.
- Simplified FAQ answers into divider rows rather than repeated framed cards.
- Moved contact, research claim boundaries and Security protection states toward solid cyan operational surfaces.
- Added scoped focus, hover, mobile and reduced-motion guards.

Black solid surfaces, cyan operational feedback and gold reserved for brand identity.

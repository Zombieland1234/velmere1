# PASS323 — Public Provenance Drop Concierge Gate

## Product delta

PASS323 adds a customer-facing **Provenance Drop Concierge** for Home, Shop, Product, Cart and Checkout. It compresses the latest public-storefront cleanup into one calm luxury rail:

- product path,
- fit visibility,
- material clarity,
- delivery/return visibility,
- quiet waitlist or receipt salon.

The public UI does not expose raw provenance scores, MEXC-style source freshness internals, PoR snapshot details, DPP scoring internals or pass identifiers as customer copy.

## Research basis

- MEXC websocket market streams require live-source expiry discipline because connections are time-limited and need reconnect handling.
- MEXC Proof-of-Reserves is treated as a transparent reserve snapshot context, not as a safety guarantee.
- LVMH/Aura Digital Product Passport patterns inform the traceability/authenticity/customer-experience direction, but public copy stays short and product-first.

## UI innovation

**Provenance Drop Concierge**: a tiny atelier concierge layer that makes elite status earned and quiet. It creates status without aggressive FOMO:

- `quiet_atelier`
- `verified_waitlist`
- `receipt_salon`
- `withheld`

## Safety boundary

No countdowns, fake scarcity, wallet pressure, buy/sell commands, ROI language, guaranteed safety wording or public raw audit payloads.
